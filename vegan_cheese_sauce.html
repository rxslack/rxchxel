My vegan cheese sauce recipe

Who doesn't need more cheeze in their lives, even the vegans...

ingredients needed:

  • plain white flour - a couple of tablespoons
  • 50g of 'butter'
  • A cup of milk - i use oat
  • 150-200g cheese of choice (We have applewood or violife pre-grated in normally, and will use a combo of both)
  • garlic - both fresh (2 cloves) and/or powder (2 tbsp) work.
  • black pepper - to taste
  • Mustard - of choice and whatever is your preference.
  • nutritional yeast - 10g goes a long way

steps:

  1. prep all the ingredients. Measuring cups can help, but i tend to eyeball.
  2. peel the garlic, but donâ€™t chop it.
  3. Melt the butter in a saucepan, and grate in the garlic cloves. let the garlic fry a little first.
  4. Add in the flour, and make the roux. i recommend a whisk, should all combine in together.
  5. start to add milk of choice slowly, combining in until smooth, and all milk combined.
  6. Add in other ingredients, let all the cheese melt until smooth
  7. either pour over pasta of choice, or keep in a container in the fridge/freezer to enjoy later
